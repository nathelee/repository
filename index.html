About Me

Hello everyone, I'm Nathalie, I started in the world of UX in the middle of this year 2021, I've loved everything I've learned, especially the importance of designing always thinking about the user and especially in the user groups that throughout history have been marginalized, discriminated or have not been represented in the designs of many products. The issue of accessibility when designing is interesting to me because I think that thinking about all kinds of users can open the doors to many cultures and learn to be more empathetic and equitable.

I love to participate as a volunteer and I have done it in different events related to the tech sector, being a volunteer has allowed me to meet amazing people, strengthen my soft skills by facing challenges such as public speaking, teamwork, etc.

I love dogs, I have one named Dante, he was rescued 3 years ago and he is my whole furry life :3
